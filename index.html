About Shadow of the Core

Shadow of the Core is a community‑driven platform designed to help creatives and entrepreneurs bring their ideas into the world. We believe in the power of collaboration and transparency to transform concepts into reality. Through our funding model, backers support projects they believe in while creators maintain full control over their work.

How It Works

1. Discover

Explore unique and inspiring projects across multiple categories.

2. Support

Select a project, choose a pledge level, and support it with a monetary contribution.

3. Follow

Receive updates from creators and follow the progress of projects you’ve backed.

4. Celebrate

Enjoy the satisfaction of helping bring a project to life and receive rewards based on your pledge level.
